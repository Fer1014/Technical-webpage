About us

At FERRDD Industries, we specialize in developing powerful software solutions designed to transform how infrastructure inspections are conducted. Our core focus is on creating advanced algorithms that analyze data captured by UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles), ensuring accurate, timely assessments of structural integrity. Partnering with industry leaders in hardware development, we equip our clients with UAVs that integrate seamlessly with our software, reducing the risks and costs associated with traditional inspection methods.

Our Values

At FERRDD Industries, we value innovation, safety, and precision, creating cutting-edge UAV solutions for efficient, risk-free infrastructure inspections. We foster collaboration, sustainability, and integrity, ensuring trustworthy, eco-friendly results that drive progress.



Objective



Our mission at FERRDD Industries is simple: To enhance the safety and efficiency of infrastructure maintenance by leveraging the power of UAV technology. We aim to provide a reliable and precise solution for inspecting hard-to-reach structures, ensuring that damage is identified early and repairs are addressed before they become critical issues. With our cutting-edge analysis tools, we empower engineers to make smarter decisions, faster.

Our Drones!

At "FERRDD Industries", we design cutting-edge drones tailored for advanced infrastructure inspections. Our flagship drone, Atlas, is built for extended flight durations, equipped with high-tech sensors and cameras to scan tall buildings and hard-to-reach areas with precision. Additionally, our indoor inspection drone is specially designed to navigate through tight spaces within structures, identifying internal damage with minimal disruption. These drones work seamlessly with our proprietary software to provide detailed, real-time data, ensuring safe and efficient infrastructure assessments.
