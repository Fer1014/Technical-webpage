About us

At FERRDD Industries, we specialize in developing powerful software solutions designed to transform how infrastructure inspections are conducted. Our core focus is on creating advanced UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) with tools and sensors to assess and evaluate the structural integrity of tough-to-reach infrastructure as well as algorithms that analyze data captured by UAVs, ensuring accurate, timely assessments of structural integrity.

Our Values

At FERRDD Industries, we value innovation, safety, and precision, creating cutting-edge UAV solutions for efficient, risk-free infrastructure inspections. We foster collaboration and team work, sustainability, and integrity, ensuring trustworthy, eco-friendly results that drive progress.


Mission


Our mission aims to transform infrastructure inspection and maintenance by delivering advanced UAV software solutions that enhance safety, precision, and efficiency for hard-to-reach environments.


Vision


Our vision is to become the global leader in UAV-based structural assessment solutions, setting the benchmark for safety, accuracy, and efficiency in infrastructure maintenance.
